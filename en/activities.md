# Summer Guide

Summer around Køyribu is all about easy access to mountain walks, cool water, quiet evenings, and flexible day trips. This page brings the best activity ideas together so you can use the cabin as a base and fill your days without much planning.

<a class="eyebrow-link" href="hikes">Continue to hikes</a>

<div class="callout">
  <p><strong>Quick overview:</strong> Start close to the cabin around Hodlekve and Dalalaven, plan swimming or fishing around the weather, and use Sogndal or Solvorn when you want a short scenic drive as part of the day.</p>
</div>

<h2 id="hiking">Start with the mountains</h2>

If hiking is your main reason for visiting, the natural place to begin is the [Hikes](hikes) page. It includes route ideas near the cabin, from easy family walks to more demanding summit trips with fjord views.

<div class="info-grid">
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Short and easy</h3>
    <p>Fagreggi and the Anestolen area work well for half-day outings, fishing, and relaxed trips with children.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Best views</h3>
    <p>Togga, Molden, and Skredfjellet give you more elevation, broader views, and a stronger summit-hike feel.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Flexible day</h3>
    <p>Gunvordalen and the nearby Hodlekve area are good options when you want to keep the day open and adapt to the weather or energy level.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<h2 id="fishing">Fishing</h2>

Fishing works especially well on calm summer days, particularly if you want something that suits both adults and children.

<div class="info-grid">
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Mountain lakes</h3>
    <p>Dalavatnet and Anestolsvatnet are larger lakes that are easy to reach by car, while Fagreggivatn is a good option if you want to combine fishing with a short walk.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Useful to know</h3>
    <p>Adults need fishing permits in the mountain lakes. Children under 16 usually fish for free. Dalalaven and Sogndal Skisenter are good places for local advice.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Fjord fishing</h3>
    <p>Around Sogndal town it is sometimes possible to catch lyre from shore, while Fjærlandsfjorden can be a good choice when mackerel are in season.</p>
  </div>
</div>

Salmon fishing in rivers is more regulated and usually requires separate permits.

<h2 id="swimming">Swimming and cool-water stops</h2>

On warm summer days there are several good places to swim or cool down, both near town and in quieter surroundings.

<ul class="link-list">
  <li>Sjøkanten in Sogndal is an easy option if you are already in town.</li>
  <li>Eide, around 10 minutes from Sogndal, is a popular swimming spot with easy access.</li>
  <li>Solvorn is worth the drive if you want a fjord-side stop in especially beautiful surroundings.</li>
  <li>The river above Hodlekve ski center is very cold, but refreshing on hot days.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="hodlekve">Dalalaven and the Hodlekve area</h2>

Dalalaven is a useful stop for both practical information and easy summer activities close to the cabin. It is often the simplest place to begin if you want a good day out without much planning.

<div class="info-grid">
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Practical first stop</h3>
    <p>The cafe, meeting point, and local maps for hiking and fishing make Dalalaven a natural starting point for many guests.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Good for families</h3>
    <p>Frisbee golf, a bike pump track, and short outing options make the area a nice fit when you want activity without committing to a full mountain day.</p>
  </div>
</div>
