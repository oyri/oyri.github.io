# Hikes Near Køyribu

Køyribu is well placed for summer hikes in the Sogndal mountains and the Fjærland area. This page gathers the routes that work especially well from the cabin, from easy family outings and fishing stops to scenic summits with broad fjord views.

<a class="eyebrow-link" href="activities">Back to activities</a>

<div class="callout">
  <p><strong>Quick overview:</strong> Choose Fagreggi or Anestolen for an easy day outdoors, Gunvordalen when you want a calmer and more open plan, and Togga, Molden, or Skredfjellet when the goal is a bigger hike with stronger views.</p>
</div>

<h2 id="nearby">Near the cabin</h2>

<div class="route-list">
  <div class="route-card">
    <div>
      <h3>Fagreggi</h3>
      <p>An easy walk up toward the treeline with several small lakes along the way. This is a very good choice if you want to stay close to the cabin area and combine a walk with fishing, breaks, and open mountain views.</p>
      <ul class="meta-list">
        <li><strong>Access:</strong> Can be walked directly from the cabin area or from Hodlekve.</li>
        <li><strong>Duration:</strong> Around 1 hour uphill, with options to continue farther.</li>
        <li><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy.</li>
        <li><strong>Fishing:</strong> Several small trout lakes. Fishing permits can be bought with Vipps `699351`. Children under 16 fish for free.</li>
        <li><strong>Links:</strong> <a href="https://www.norgeskart.no/#!?project=norgeskart&layers=1001&zoom=13&lat=6819263.65&lon=69324.80&sok=fagreggi&markerLat=6819267.220029058&markerLon=69071.47161072219&p=searchOptionsPanel">Map on Norgeskart</a> and <a href="https://www.visitnorway.no/listings/g%C3%A5tur-til-fagreggi/261753">route info on Visit Norway</a>.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="image-frame">
      <img src="../img/hikes/fagereggi-vatn-regn.jpeg" alt="Fagreggi with small mountain lakes">
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="route-card">
    <div>
      <h3>Gunvordalen</h3>
      <p>If you would rather choose a flatter and easier outing, Gunvordalen is a good fit. The terrain is gentler than the surrounding summits, and it works well when you want a walk without turning the whole day into a major hike.</p>
      <ul class="meta-list">
        <li><strong>Start point:</strong> Same parking area as Togga.</li>
        <li><strong>Duration:</strong> 1 to 3 hours round trip depending on how far you go.</li>
        <li><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy.</li>
        <li><strong>Link:</strong> <a href="https://peakbook.org/no/peakbook-element/63255/Tursti+Gunvordalen.html">Gunvordalen trail info on Peakbook</a>.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="image-frame">
      <img src="../img/hikes/gjerde-sogndal-20221203_140809.jpg" alt="Gentler mountain terrain in the Gunvordalen area">
    </div>

<h2 id="family-hikes">Family hikes and easy outings</h2>

<div class="route-list">
  <div class="route-card">
    <div>
      <h3>Anestolen</h3>
      <p>A family-friendly valley outing with goats in the summer grazing area and good opportunities for both fishing and swimming. This is one of the most flexible options in the area, especially for families or days with mixed energy levels.</p>
      <ul class="meta-list">
        <li><strong>Drive time:</strong> About 10 minutes from the cabin via the toll road from Hodlekve.</li>
        <li><strong>Duration:</strong> 1 to 2 hours round trip if you walk the gravel road, or a shorter stop if you drive in.</li>
        <li><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy.</li>
        <li><strong>Extra:</strong> Good for fishing, swimming, and seeing animals. The toll road is around NOK 50 per car.</li>
        <li><strong>Links:</strong> <a href="https://www.fjordnorway.com/no/se-og-gjore/sykkeltur-til-anestolen">Anestolen on Fjord Norway</a> and <a href="https://www.visitnorway.no/listings/guida-st%C3%B8lsbes%C3%B8k-anest%C3%B8len-sogndal/245705/">guided farm visit info</a>.</li>
      </ul>

<h2 id="summits">Summit hikes with views</h2>

<div class="route-list">
  <div class="route-card">
    <div>
      <h3>Togga</h3>
      <p>A popular summit close to the cabin with broad views once you are above the forest. The climb is steep, so this route is best on dry days when you want a more demanding outing.</p>
      <ul class="meta-list">
        <li><strong>Drive time:</strong> 5 to 10 minutes to the trailhead at Nystolen.</li>
        <li><strong>Duration:</strong> 3 to 4 hours round trip.</li>
        <li><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Demanding.</li>
        <li><strong>Note:</strong> Can be slippery in wet conditions near the forest line.</li>
        <li><strong>Links:</strong> <a href="https://ut.no/turforslag/116454">Route description on UT.no</a> and <a href="https://www.fjellvenner.no/fjellturer-i-andre-fjellomraader/togga-1205-moh">photos and inspiration</a>.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="image-frame">
      <img src="../img/hikes/mot-rindabotn-20220806_150411.jpg" alt="Mountain terrain near Togga">
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="route-card">
    <div>
      <h3>Molden</h3>
      <p>A classic regional hike with impressive views over Lustrafjord. It works well as a day trip from the cabin when you want a moderate hike paired with one of the best-known fjord panoramas in the area.</p>
      <ul class="meta-list">
        <li><strong>Drive time:</strong> About 30 minutes to the trailhead at Krossen.</li>
        <li><strong>Duration:</strong> 2 to 3 hours round trip.</li>
        <li><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium.</li>
        <li><strong>Link:</strong> <a href="https://ut.no/turforslag/118573/topptur-til-molden-fra-krossen">Molden route on UT.no</a>.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="image-frame">
      <img src="../img/hikes/molden.jpeg" alt="Molden with views over Lustrafjord">
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="route-card">
    <div>
      <h3>Skredfjellet</h3>
      <p>A rewarding hike toward Fjærland if you want both fjord and mountain scenery in the same trip. Starting from Berge makes it a slightly bigger excursion from the cabin, but the views from the top are worth it.</p>
      <ul class="meta-list">
        <li><strong>Drive time:</strong> About 20 minutes from the cabin.</li>
        <li><strong>Duration:</strong> 3 to 4 hours round trip.</li>
        <li><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Medium.</li>
        <li><strong>Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.visitnorway.no/listings/fjell-og-fjord-vandring-til-skredfjellet-i-fj%C3%A6rland/246118/">Route description on Visit Norway</a>.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>

<h2 id="before-you-go">Tips before you head out</h2>

<div class="info-grid">
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Plan around the weather</h3>
    <p>Check the forecast before you leave, especially for Togga and Skredfjellet. Steeper sections change character quickly in wet weather.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Bring a little extra</h3>
    <p>Water, extra layers, and sturdy shoes make a big difference. Some stretches are rocky, and mountain weather changes fast.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Maps and permits</h3>
    <p>Use <a href="https://ut.no/">UT.no</a> and <a href="https://www.norgeskart.no/">Norgeskart</a> for planning. Fishing permits are available through <a href="https://www.sogndalskisenter.no/fiske">Sogndal Skisenter</a>.</p>
  </div>
</div>

Happy hiking from all of us at Køyribu. If you want a recommendation based on weather, fitness level, or who is joining, we are happy to share more local suggestions.
