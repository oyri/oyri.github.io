# The Cabin

Koyribu is a spacious mountain cabin above Sogndal, designed for calm days, long summer evenings, and easy access to some of the best nature experiences in the area.

[See practical guest information](guest-info)

---

## A Comfortable Summer Base

The cabin works well for families, couples, and small groups who want a peaceful place to stay while exploring Sogndal, Hodlekve, and Fjærland. It is especially well suited for summer stays with hiking, swimming, fishing, and day trips nearby.

- 4 bedrooms with several sleeping spaces
- 2 bathrooms with shower and toilet
- Fully equipped kitchen
- WiFi, bedding, and towels for booked guests
- Spacious social areas indoors and outdoors

---

## What the Cabin Feels Like

Koyribu is more than a practical place to sleep. It is a family cabin with room to gather, cook, rest, and spend time together after a day outside. The setting is quiet and sheltered, with nature close by in every direction.

The cabin is a good fit if you want:

- a calm base in the mountains
- easy access to summer hikes
- space for family meals and slow mornings
- a stay that feels private rather than crowded

---

## Inside the Cabin

The indoor spaces are generous and functional, with an open living area, a well-equipped kitchen, and several bedrooms that make longer stays comfortable. There is also a sauna, practical storage, and room for both quiet time and shared meals.
